Our Technology

We combine cutting-edge technologies to make it possible for you to control your environment with your mind.

  • Smart Headband: EEG/EMG Sensors for detecting neural signals.
  • AR Glasses: Custom AR apps for immersive interaction.
  • IoT Devices: ESP32 and Raspberry Pi for smart device control.
  • Brain & Gesture Control: Mediapipe for eye-tracking and gesture recognition.
  • Voice/Text AI: GPT-based chatbot for communication.
  • Computer Vision: OpenCV for visual processing in AR environments.

Challenges

Auralink faces several challenges in its development and implementation:

  • Technical Issues: Extracting clear brain signals, real-time processing, and hardware usability.
  • Medical & Safety Concerns: Biocompatibility, long-term safety, and patient adaptability.
  • Regulatory & Ethical Hurdles: Navigating FDA approvals, data privacy, and ethical considerations.
  • User Experience: Ensuring ease of use, seamless training, and affordability.
